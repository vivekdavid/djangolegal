Winning a DUI case


Winning a drunk driving case is usually focused on the technical requirements of the breath test. For example, the police must show that the machine was properly maintained and calibrated.   They must show they gave you your rights to counsel and they did two breath tests 20 minutes apart.   There are a host of other requirements that the police must meet that a lawyer can review for you.
