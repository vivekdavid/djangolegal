Mischief


How to get a mischief charge dropped.


If the mischief involved only little damage or obstruction to property, a mischief charge can be dropped with probation and a discharge.   A discharge is not a conviction but will show up on your record as a discharge.   It will be on your record for 1 to 3 years depending on the type of discharge you get.  

If the mischief is more serious, a conditional sentence may be available which is a finding of guilt but does not involve prison time or fine.
